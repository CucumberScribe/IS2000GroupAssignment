Our mission statement:

Needy animals are given a safe haven filled with love and care at the Iron County Humane Animal Shelter. Finding loving homes for rescued animals is our unwavering mission. We strive to promote responsible pet ownership and raise awareness within local communities with the aim of establishing a world where animals are appreciated and treasured. We believe collective effort can bring about change.
