Meet Louis

Breed: Miniature Pinscher/Pug

Age: 1

Sex: Male

Color: Tan

Weight: 15 lbs

Louis is an adorable pug/miniature pinscher mix (Also called a "Muggins"). He was rescued from a farm and is now eagerly searching for his forever home. Louis is extremely playful and active and loves playing fetch and going for long walks and hikes. Louis gets along well with kids and other dogs, making him a great addition to any family. However, he is not overly familiar with cats yet. By adopting Louis, you would be inviting a loyal and affectionate companion into your home!
