Who Are We?

We are a community funded shelter dedicated to housing and caring for as many homeless animals as we can.

Our driving goal is to foster a community wherein each animal under our care is soon delivered to a loving home.

How Have We Accomplished Our Goal So Far?

To date, our shelter has retained a 70% adoption rate for our animals.

Funded through generous donations by our community, we strive each year to increase this number even higher!

What Do We Need?

We're always looking for new volunteers to join the dozens already striving to accomplish our goal!

If you're incapable to share with us you're time, there's many ways you can DONATE to our cause instead!
